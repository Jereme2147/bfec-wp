Environmental Permitting Write-Up

Brushy Fork has extensive experience in obtaining permits for clients, as nearly every project we complete requires some type of environmental permitting. Even projects which improve the long term health of a water body typically require permits to ensure that the project is designed and constructed in a responsible manner. Rather than risk facing fines or delays during construction, contact Brushy Fork prior to beginning any work in or around streams or wetlands to determine your permitting needs. We provide:

  • US Army Corps 404 permitting
  • State 401 permitting
  • Erosion and sediment control plans
  • No-rise certifications
  • Jurisdictional determinations
  • Trout buffer disturbance waivers
  • Reclamation plans
  • Local governmental permitting
  • Park & Trail Design and Permitting
